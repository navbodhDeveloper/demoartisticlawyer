About the Artist

Neelu Rungta

Neelu Rungta is a devoted homemaker and a lifelong artist whose love for art began in childhood. She completed her fashion designing degree, but her true passion always lay in creating expressive paintings. Over the years, she has mastered the richness of oil painting and continues to craft soulful pieces that reflect her artistic journey. Balancing home and creativity with grace, she still finds time to pour her heart onto canvas. Many of the artworks featured on this website are her beautiful creations, each one a testament to her dedication and talent.

Radhika Rungta Agrawal

Radhika Rungta Agrawal is an artist by heart and a lawyer by profession. Inspired by her mother’s beautiful paintings, she developed a deep love for art from childhood. After completing her law graduation in 2020, she rediscovered her passion during the Covid years and began creating again. What started as a personal escape soon grew into a journey of workshops, creativity, and community. Today, she blends her artistic expression with mindful experiences, sharing the joy of art with everyone who connects with her work.
